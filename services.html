What can CSP Graphics do for you?

 

Exhibition Graphics  •  Banners  •  Posters  •  P.O.P.  •  Mock-ups  •  Architectural and Interior Decoration  •  Ceramics Printing  •  Reinforced Vinyl  •  Pressure Sensitive Vinyl  •  Canvas  •  Fabrics  •  Foam Board  •  Corrugated Board  •  Lenticular  •  Tile  •  Drywall  •  Glass  •  Sheet Metal  •  Paper and more

 

Our digital flatbed presses have an 8 picoliter variable dot gray scale, at a native 600 dpi and can deliver 1200 dpi for photo-realistic image quality and fine text down to 4pt positive and negative whilst still delivering intense band free solids. We can print ridged or flexible substrates up to 122" H x 79" W (309 cm x 200 cm) and up to 2” (5 cm) in thickness.

 

CSP Graphics also offers digital white ink capability allowing you to have digitally printed backlit graphics and window clings on rigid and flexible media. White ink also gives you the ability to create specialty applications on almost any media or object. Doors, wood, glass, Plexiglas, tiles, stone, cardboard, metals - the list is endless.

 

We also offer digital cutting options such as digital routing, knife cutting, kiss cutting, V-routing, and score and crease capabilities.  CSP Graphics offers innovative, modular finishing solutions for processing anything from preprinted and unprinted pressure-sensitive vinyl/film and fabrics up to 10ft wide, to rigid materials up to 2" thick, including corrugated/composite materials, display board, plastics, acrylics, MDF, and even solid, non-ferrous metals
